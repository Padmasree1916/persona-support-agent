# Team access and roles

Adsparkx roles are Owner, Admin, Campaign Manager, Analyst, and Billing Viewer. Only Owners can transfer ownership, delete a workspace, or change bank details. Admins can invite members and manage campaigns but cannot transfer ownership.

If an invitation expired, an Admin or Owner can revoke it and send a new invitation. Invitations expire after 72 hours. Access disputes and ownership changes must be escalated for identity verification.
